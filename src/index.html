What is this?

Our slack-based community, #studio_studio, a channel in Learning Gardens, is starting a snail-mail based object exchange!

We will each create a series of objects that either fits, or can be packed down into the 11"x8.5"x5.5" Flat Rate USPS box, and then ship them to another #studio_studio member to assemble and document.
